Help Window

If the game complains about your window size.. Hold Control and hit - to give it more space

The goal of this game is to defend your paulville from annoying 'friends' every round by building paul turrets.

The top of the screen are your resources -- you need these to build pauls.

At the bottom are options for what pauls you can build. Clicking one of these buttons will show you(in the bottom left) the name of the paul, the damage, and the resource needs.

With a paul choice selected, you can click on a block on the field to build that paul. This will consume resources. Clicking a paul on the battlefield lets you see it's stats and destroy it if you want to

Pauls will try to target the first enemy that comes within its range, so it may be wise to spread out pauls so that they target different enemies.

A Paul's range only depends on horizontal distance, Pauls on lower parts of the grid do not have a disadvantage.
